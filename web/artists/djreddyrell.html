DJ REDDY RELL

     

DJ Reddy Rell, aka “The Crossover King,” reps a small group of cities between New York and Philadelphia called Lehigh Valley, PA. He has an internationally recognized career and is well known for his massive productions within the mixtape game where he fuses different sounds and techniques. DJ Reddy Rell masterfully commands various genres in his queue, including Hip-Hop, R&B, Dancehall, House, and Electro.

His mixtape series, Hip Hop TXL, is currently one of the most popular compilations in the world, averaging 150,000 downloads and 1 million views for each release. These mixes are comprised entirely of new, exclusive tracks from mainstream artists and upcoming talent from all over the world.

DJ Reddy Rell's mixtapes can be found on online music platforms such as Datpiff.com and HipHopTXL.com.
